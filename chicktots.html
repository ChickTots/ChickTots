Our Story

A snack that fits in your hand.

ChickTots started with a simple question — why are the best snacks always so messy to eat? Inspired by the grab-and-go magic of American pretzel bites and the beloved Brazilian coxinha, we built a cup-first concept designed for how people actually eat today: one-handed, on the move, between the meetings, between the games, between the errands.

Every tot is cooked to order and sent straight to your door in a grab-and-go cup. No dining room. No waiting tables. Just dangerously crispy tots, big American flavors, and a crew of characters your kids will recognize by name.
